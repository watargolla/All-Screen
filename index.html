FAQ

★ Connection Problem

I can't detect the cast device:

1. Make sure your phone and cast device are connecting to the same Wi-Fi. if you don't have router or wifi then enable hotspot mode on your phone and connect your streaming device to your hotspot.Open the Xtreme Video Cast app on mobile, If your Streaming device(or smart TV) is supported, app will find it and select it. Once casting device is selected, select any media to play on it.

2. Make sure your VPN is off on your phone.

3. Try to reboot(restart) your router, cast device or phone.

Why does the app keep getting disconnected?

It may relates to the setting of your device, so please make sure you haven't set any battery optimization, which may prevent app from functioning properly, for example, turning off Wi-Fi when the phone is not in use. If so, please turn on the setting to prevent disconnection.

I can't see anything on TV while the app performs well on my phone:

Please select the right input, if your TV supports multi-inputs: Chromecast, Xbox or Fire stick.

★ File Not Found

I can't find the file I want to cast:

App will scan the video, audio and picture automatically on your phone. Please check 'Directories' at the bottom of folders. If it still can not be found, let us know the file path and format of the file. We will optimize it.

★ Media(Video,Image & Audio) Problems

My video/audio/picture fails to play:

It may be that the format of the file is not supported by cast device.

Chromecast: https://developers.google.com/cast/docs/media

Fire TV/Fire Stick: https://developer.amazon.com/docs/fire-tv/device-specifications.html

Roku: https://sdkdocs.roku.com/display/sdkdoc/Audio+and+Video+Support

My video always pauses, buffers or lags:

It may be that the bitrate of the video is too high for the cast device to keep up and the poor network is also a cause. Please try to reboot your router, reopen All Screen and cast again.

My video plays without sound:

It may be that the audio codec of your video is not supported by cast device.

Chromecast: https://developers.google.com/cast/docs/media

Fire TV/Fire Stick: https://developer.amazon.com/docs/fire-tv/device-specifications.html
Roku: https://sdkdocs.roku.com/display/sdkdoc/Audio+and+Video+Support

My video plays sound but without picture:

It may be that the video codec of your of your video is not supported by cast device.

Chromecast: https://developers.google.com/cast/docs/media

Fire TV/Fire Stick: https://developer.amazon.com/docs/fire-tv/device-specifications.html
Roku: https://sdkdocs.roku.com/display/sdkdoc/Audio+and+Video+Support

★ Media Casting Problems

Why is there always a “No media found”?

1.Make sure the video or audio is playing in the browser.

2.Try to refresh the current page.

3.Some websites may have media protection, we can’t get resource from it directly. You can feedback to us and we will optimize it.

I can’t play the browser video:

Please ensure the video plays correctly in the browser. Your device may not support the format of the video. You can try a different video source if available.

★ Subtitle issue

Subtitles are only supported on Chromecast, Fire TV/Stick, Roku TV/Stick and supported DLNA devices
